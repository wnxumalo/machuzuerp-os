The Items To Be Ordered section shows Inventory that has reached or is below the Re-Order Level or that is out of stock. These items are monitored and updated when an Invoice or Receipt is processed.

To select an item and place it in an order, you must click on it from the Items To Be Ordered section, then click to move it to the Order section.

Items in the Order section are those that will be processed with the Purchase Order.

Click on to remove an item from the Order section.

If an item you want to order does not appear in the Items To Be Ordered section, you can select an item from your defined inventory items by clicking . This shows the screen below, which displays the contents of your Services / Inventory database.

Once you have added all items, click on

The program will locate the item in the Inventory database and notify you of the Cost of the item as shown below.

The Price / Cost shown is the cost you defined in the Services / Inventory section. You would usually mark this price up with your own price. It is the price that you purchase the goods for.

You can change this price or click OK to continue processing the order with the current price.

The program allows you to create Invoices and Receipts for items that are no longer in stock. These items are Back-Ordered and they can be recovered when processing an order. The screen below shows that the the stock level of the Maestro Invoicer is below 0 and that the program will calculate how much you need to order to get the Item to it's Re-Order level. You will have to add more of the item in addition to the amount shown to get the item to your desired level.

The screen below will show the amount required to get the Stock Level to the Re-Order Level. You must add more to this value to get to your desired level.

Click on OK to complete the order.
