SERVICE ITEMS

Click on

The screen shown below will appear.

New will allow you to define a new Service Item, Edit will allow you to edit a Service Item, Find will allow you to locate a record for viewing or editing and List will list all Service Items.

You can use the buttons to navigate the records one by one.

You must select what type of Service Item you are defining in the Type field. A service Item can either be Stock or Service. Stock is your inventory and Service is any service that you provide.

The Code is the number / Barcode of the item.

If you select Service, you must enter the Service Item's details in all the relevant fields. An item can have up to 3 rates (normal, discount, other). Not all fields have to be filled in. You will not be able to edit the Stock section.

If you select Stock, you must enter the detials of the Service Item in the Stock section at the bottom of the screen

The Cost is the amount you buy the item for and the Markup is the percentage you mark the item up by.

The program will calculate the Selling Price once the Cost and Markup have been entered. You can enter your own Selling Price.

The Reorder Level is the level at which you want the system to notify you that you are running out of stock and Stock At Hand is the amount of the item you have in stock.

Expiry Date is the date that the item will expire. This is mostly relevant to groceries/restaurants where food will expire. The program will notify you that this item has expired before an invoice or receipt is created for it.

Once all the details have been entered, click on
