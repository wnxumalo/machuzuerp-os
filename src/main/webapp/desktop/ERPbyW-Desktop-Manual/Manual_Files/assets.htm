Assets



Clicking on

The screen below will appear.

New will allow you to define a new asset, Find will locate an asset's record in the database, Notes will allow you to enter notes for the asset, Assets will show a report concerning all assets.

Enter your Asset's detalis in all the relevant fields.

Purchase Cost is the amount you purchase the asset for. Current Price is the current value of the asset. This value will be automatically updated by the program. Depreciation % is the percentage that the asset depreciates by. If the asset appreciates, enter a negative value in the field.

Depreciation Frequency is how often the asset appreciates or depreciates.

The Age-Months and Age-Years will automatically be updated by the program as time passes. You can enter 0 for both fields when the asset is new.

In the Loan section, you can enter the loan details if relevant.

The Amount is the loan amount, Loan Due is the amount that is due. The Loan Paid is the amount that has already been paid. Months is the number of months to pay the loan off.

The program will automatically calculate the Monthly installment.

To make a payment to the loan, click on Pay. The Loan Paid, Loan Due and Remaining Installments are automatically updated after a payment is made for the loan.

Once all details have been entered, click on
