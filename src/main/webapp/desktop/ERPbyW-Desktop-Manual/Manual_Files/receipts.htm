RECEIPTS

NB: All Totals for Receipts are written to the Trial Balance report.

To create a receipt, click on the button. The screen below will appear.

The customers which you have saved in the program will appear in the Stakeholders section. Please see the Stakeholders section for more information about setting up customers.

Enter the customer's name in the Search field or scroll up or down the list to locate their record in the program.

Double click on the customer in the list to select them or click on the customer, then click .

You can simply enter the customer's details in the fields. ie: you can simply enter the Client Name, Telephone etc without having the customer's details saved in the program.

You can simply enter the Amount Due and Amount Received for the receipt then save without selecting what service / inventory the receipt is for.

The Services you provide and Inventory / Stock which you have saved in the program will appear in the Services / Inventory section. Please see the Services section for more information about setting up services and inventory.

Enter the Service or Inventory Item's code/barcode in the Search By No. field , or enter the Service or Inventory Item's name/description in the Search By Name field or scroll up or down the list to locate the record in the program.

Double click on the Service or Inventory Item in the list to select it or click on the Service or Inventory Item, then click .

You can simply enter the Service / Inventory Item's details in the fields without having it saved in the database. ie: you can simply enter the Number(Code/Barcode), Description, Unit Price etc without having the Service / Inventory Item's details saved in the program.

The program will automatically add 1 of the Item or Service to the Receipt after double clicking the Service or Inventory or clicking .

Before adding Inventory to an Receipt, the program will check how much of the Item is available in stock. You will be notified if the Item is running out (When Re-Order Level has been reached) and when it has run out.

Some Items may expire, such as in groceries. You can set the expiry date in the Services section to be notified. You do not HAVE TO set this date. If the date is not set the message that appears when adding an item to an receipt notifying that the item has expired should be ignored.

This will add the service item to the receipt. You will see the added services as on the screen below

You may select the Payment Method for it to appear on the receipt.

Once all the items have been added and the receipt is completed, click on

The receipt can be partially paid for. The outstanding amount will be kept on record. To clear the receipt, a Receipt will have to be processed.

If you would like to view or print the receipt immediately after creating it, click Yes when the program asks if the receipt is a Point of Sale.

NB- Make sure that all the items you are creating the receipt for are are shown in the added Items screen before saving, that the customer data is correct and that the tax box has been ticked to charge tax if applicable.

To view and print an receipt, click on Reports - View Receipts

Next, click on Reports - Preview Receipt, then type in the number of the receipt you want to print or view.
