Services

Two type of Services can be defined:
i) Service
ii) Stock

Services are your consultations, tooth fillings, extractions etc. Stock is the material you keep in stock and use in surgery etc. For example, a vernier is considered as stock.

Refer to to the image below for a stock example.

Cost is the amount it cost you to buy the material / item, Markup is the percentage you are increasing the cost of the material / item by. Sale is the amount you will be selling the item for. This amount is auto-calculated when you enter the field. It is based on the Cost and Markup. Reorder level is the level at which you would like to be notified that you are running out of stock. Stock At Hand is the amount of stock you have. Everytime you prescribe / use some stock it is deducted from the stock. When you reach the reorder level you will be notified of that.
